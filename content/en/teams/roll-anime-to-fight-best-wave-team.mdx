<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Merge and Level Fighters">

The Merge system is the primary way to increase your combat power without relying solely on luck. To build the **roll anime to fight best wave team**, you must understand the efficiency of merging duplicates.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Main Copy">
    Check your inventory for the unit with the best base stats or mutation (e.g., a Titan version). This will be your 'Base' unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Filter Matching Duplicates">
    Locate all standard or lower-value versions of that same character. Ensure you aren't using a unit that is currently equipped in your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Merge">
    Use the in-game merge interface to consume the duplicates. This will increase the Level, DPS, and sometimes improve the RNG (Range) of your main copy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Performance">
    Enter a wave run to see if the upgraded unit now outperforms other members of your team. If it does, prioritize future merges for this unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Merge Level | Stat Increase (Approx) | Resource Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1-5** | +25% Total DPS | 4 Duplicates |
| **Level 6-10** | +60% Total DPS | 10 Duplicates |
| **Level 11+** | +120% Total DPS | 20+ Duplicates |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
Once a unit is consumed in a merge, it is gone forever. Double-check that you are not accidentally merging a rare mutation into a standard unit. Always lock your best fighters!
</Callout>
