<Section icon={Target} title="How to Merge and Level Fighters">

Upgrading your units is the only way to survive the scaling difficulty of endless waves. The merge system allows you to combine identical units, translating duplicate rolls into permanent power increases.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Base Fighter">
    Choose the fighter with the highest base rarity or the best active mutation to act as the primary unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Isolate Mutation Branches">
    Keep Gold, Diamond, Nen, and Titan variants separate. Never merge a premium mutation into a standard copy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Matching Duplicates">
    Roll for extra copies of your equipped units. Standard duplicates are highly valuable as leveling food.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Merge">
    Use the in-game merge menu to feed duplicate copies into your selected base unit, raising its level and DPS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
Focus your merges on one or two core damage dealers first. A single high-level carry is often more effective than an evenly leveled team of weak units.
</Callout>
