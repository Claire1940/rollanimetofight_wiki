<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Process">

To optimize your team effectively, follow this structured approach to merging. This ensures your resources are spent on the fighters that provide the highest return on investment (ROI) for wave progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Carry">
    Check your roster for the unit with the highest **DPS (Damage Per Second)**. This is usually a Mythic or a highly mutated Legendary. This unit will receive 70% of your merge resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Filter for Mutations">
    Separate your units by mutation type. If you have a Gold version and a Normal version of the same character, the Gold version is your "Base."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bulk Roll for Fodder">
    Spend your currency on rolls to collect "Standard Duplicates." These are the identical characters without mutations that serve as the "Food" for your main carries.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Merge">
    Open the merge menu, select your Mutated/Mythic base, and add the standard duplicates. Confirm that the level and DPS numbers increase before finalizing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test and Re-evaluate">
    Take your newly leveled unit into a wave run. If it still struggles to clear enemies, consider if you need more **AOE coverage** or if you should pivot your merge focus to a different unit type.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The cost of merging increases as the unit's level rises. It is often more efficient to have three Level 5 units than one Level 10 unit during the early game.
</Callout>

</Section>
