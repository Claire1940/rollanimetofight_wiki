<Section icon={Swords} title="Merging and Leveling Guide">

Merging is the primary method for increasing the raw power of your anime fighters. By combining duplicate units, you increase the level of your "Main" copy. This process is straightforward but requires discipline to ensure you aren't wasting rare resources on units you will eventually replace.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Main Copy">
    Check your inventory for the version of the fighter with the best mutation (Titan > Nen > Diamond > Gold > Standard). This unit will receive all future merges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Filter Duplicates">
    Collect matching duplicates from your rolls. Ensure these duplicates do not have a better mutation than your current main unit before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Merge">
    Open the merge menu, select your Main unit, and feed the duplicates into it. This will increase the unit's level and combat stats like DPS and Range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Stat Growth">
    Compare the newly leveled unit against your current team. If the upgraded stats exceed your lowest-performing team member, swap them out immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The efficiency of your merges depends on your focus. It is better to have one Level 50 Mythic than five Level 10 Mythics. Concentrated power allows you to break through the "health walls" that enemies develop in later waves.

| Merge Level | Stat Increase | Material Required | Efficiency Rating |
