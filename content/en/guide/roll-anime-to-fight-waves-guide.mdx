To maximize your efficiency during wave runs, follow this structured progression loop. This pathway prevents resource waste and ensures your team's damage scales smoothly alongside enemy health pools.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Roll Your First Roster">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Balance Single-Target & AOE">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Isolate Mutated Branches">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Merge Standard Duplicates">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Push to the Next Checkpoint">
    Enter the waves and push as far as possible. If you fail, analyze which units fell behind in damage, upgrade them, and attempt the run again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
